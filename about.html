About Us

Cynthia's Succulents, based in Toronto, is a boutique shop specializing in custom succulent arrangements and installations for homes and businesses across the Greater Toronto Area. We focus on creating unique, sustainable plant designs that bring a touch of nature into any space, perfectly suited for the diverse and vibrant environment of Toronto.

Our projects primarily consist of Public and Commercial installations. Public projects include schools, universities, libraries, hospitals, museums, and community centers. Commercial projects include hotels, office buildings, restaurants, retail stores, and cafes. Private projects include homes, apartments, and condominiums.

Cynthia's Succulents operates out of two facilities in Toronto: a 7,200 sq. ft. greenhouse and an additional 5,000 sq. ft. studio for custom arrangements and workshops.

We handle projects ranging from $150 to $1,800 and manage a portfolio of 20 to 30 projects per year. Cynthia's Succulents is certified by the Toronto Green Standard and is committed to sustainable practices. Furthermore, we collaborate with local artists and horticulturists to ensure the highest quality and creativity in our designs.
